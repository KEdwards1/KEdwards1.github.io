About Me

Hello I'm Karlyce. I'm a native New Yorker and I love video games. Before GA I was a technology coordinator at a graduate school.

I came to GA because the experience of helping build a MOOC made inspired me to move from supporting user applications to building them.

One of the major things that appeals to me about web development is the challenge of turning code into something useable by others. My goal with attending the WDI program is not only to work on software inside of an organization, but to also create my own web applications.

According to Buzzfeed, my spirit animal is a sloth.
